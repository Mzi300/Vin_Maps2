# VinMaps: Search & Discovery Intelligence System Roadmap

VinMaps is an AI-powered South African navigation, safety, transport, and road intelligence platform. It functions as a navigation system, local business discovery system, transport intelligence system, and safety awareness system.

## Phase 1 — Location Search Engine
- Intelligent search for streets, townships, suburbs, and landmarks.
- Features: Fast autocomplete, search history, multi-language support.

## Phase 2 — Business & Services Search
- Categories: Shisanyama, Spaza shops, Taxi ranks, Gautrain stations.
- Features: Open status, safety score, crowd activity estimation.

## Phase 3 — Road Intelligence Search
- Real-time search for accidents, potholes, hijack hotspots, and load shedding robots.

## Phase 4 — Community Reporting System
- Real-time reporting for protests, taxi strikes, and service delivery issues.

## Phase 5 — Natural Language AI Search
- Conversational search: "nearest clinic open now", "safe petrol station nearby".

## Phase 6 — South African Localization
- Understanding "robot", "garage", "rank", "kasi", and "shisanyama".

## Phase 7 — Smart Search Ranking
- Ranking by Safety Score, Traffic, and Community Trust (not just distance).

## Phase 8 — Search Filters
- Filters: Safest, Cheapest, Diesel available, Low queues.

## Phase 9 — Voice Search System
- AI voice recognition for South African accents and pronunciation.

## Phase 10 — Public Transport Intelligence
- Taxi routes, fares, and rank congestion heatmaps.

## Phase 11 — Safety Intelligence System
- Safe route mode, night safety mode, and crime avoidance routing.

## Phase 12 — Weather & Environmental Search
- Flood warnings, fog zones, and dynamic rerouting.

## Phase 13 — Offline Search System
- Offline maps for townships, emergency services, and taxi ranks.

## Phase 14 — Autocomplete & Predictive Search
- AI intent prediction and behavior learning.

## Phase 15 — AI Recommendation Engine
- Personalized safest routes and best nearby shisanyama.

## Phase 16 — System Architecture
- Scalable Node.js/Laravel backend, PostGIS, WebSockets, and AI inference.
